Taught my self to code Calculus functions via volatility for a currency trading bot

To the right is a self built function that takes in a list full of prices and the times that price itself was taken.

The function will then go through the list, calculate the volatility price, which is (current price - average price)^2, And then proceeds to divide by the length of the list up until that price point. The result is the derivative AKA (in terms of finance) as the volatility.

We then do this for multiple time points, such as 5 min, 15 min, 1 hour, 3 hour, 6 hour, 12 hour, 1 doy, 2 day, and 1 week

But if there is only one item in the list, then we set all of our volatility numbers to 0 so as to avoid confusion in the other logic of our program
